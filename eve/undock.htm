iterami/Guides.htm/EVE Online/Undock
  • Always have instadock and instaundock bookmarks for the station you are camping.
  • High security trade hub main stations are the best locations for undock camping.
  • If you have to choose between multiple targets to have selected: first choose the ship with the highest estimated price, then choose the ship with the highest bounty. If all choices are the same, choose the one with the lowest remaining EHP.
  • Players can bump you away from station docking range, so be careful when accepting duels or shooting ships that won't have criminal status for more than ~5 minutes.
  • Send a duel request to players that are self-destructing their capsules.
  • Set other campers to -5 or -10 to keep track of them and more easily notice targets and competition.
  • Set your weapon "Auto-Repeat" to off to avoid missed shots caused by failure to deactivate your weapon.
  • You should use a ship with a lot of EHP in order to be essentially immune to any nearby ganking ships.
