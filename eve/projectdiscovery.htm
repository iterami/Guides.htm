Hunting for Exoplanets:
  • Each character on your account can independently complete Project Discovery.
  • Keep your accuracy rating as high as possible, for you will get bigger ISK rewards. Every 10% accuracy greatly increases your ISK rewards.
  • Rewards must be redeemed while at a station.
  • There is no penalty for closing the window after you have already seen the sample. You can reopen it to get a different sample that may be easier to successfully complete.
  • You can continue Project Discovery even after rank 500.
