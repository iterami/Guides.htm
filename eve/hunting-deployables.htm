  • Steps to find and kill a deployable:
    1. Find a deployable object to kill via your directional scanner. Any ship can be used.
    2. Scan and bookmark the deployable using a scanning ship. Current best scanning fit: [Heron, SCANNING-HIGHSEC-CHEAP]
    3. Return to the bookmark with a DPS ship and destroy it. Current best DPS fit: [Coercer, DPS-CHEAP]
