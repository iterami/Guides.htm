  • "Drone Danger" indicates if enemy ships will switch to targeting your drones after they have already started attacking your ship. Destroying certain classes of enemy ships can allow you to use bigger drones:
    1. Small drones are usually attacked by enemy frigates.
    2. Medium drones are usually attacked by enemy friagtes and destroyers.
    3. Heavy drones are usually attacked by enemy friagtes, destroyers, and cruisers.
  • Some missions may required specific items or modules. Keep them in your mission station until needed:
    • Angel Palladium Tag for the Angel Extravaganza mission final gate.
    • Miner II for missions that require you to mine ore.
