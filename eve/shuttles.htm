All players should use one of the four playable race shuttles. Each racial shuttle has +10% armor resistance to a specific damage type. Choose the shuttle that most greatly resists the damage type you are most likely to encounter.
Amarr +10% Explosive Armor Resistance
Caldari +10% Thermal Armor Resistance
Gallente +10% Kinetic Armor Resistance
Minmatar +10% EM Armor Resistance
Other shuttles are not worth flying due to their significantly increased cost and rarity.
