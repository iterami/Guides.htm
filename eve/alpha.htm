  • Never use Cerebral Accelerators to boost your attributes. You will max your skillpoints at 5 million within a few months of constant training, so it is always better to keep them or sell them. Boosted attributes do not help you when you can only gain skillpoints from Daily Alpha Injectors.
  • Alpha clones only get 5 million skill points before they must purchase expensive Daily Alpha Injectors.
