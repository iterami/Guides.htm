iterami/Guides.htm/EVE Online/General
  • Always have your skill training and science queues as full as possible.
  • Autopilot:
    • Initiate your first warp before enabling autopilot.
    • Never use autopilot when flying through low/null security space, when flying valuable ships, or when flying ships with valuable cargo.
  • Do not accept missions that you cannot complete before the Bonus Reward timer expires.
  • Drones should remain in the drone bay until they are needed to attack something.
  • Move your assets to a single system for easier management and tracking, unless strategic asset placement is required.
  • Keep ship price low when fitting:
    • Avoid social interactions that may lead to a bounty being placed on your head.
    • Keep your cargohold as empty as possible by dropping off acquired loot at a station.
    • Never fly special edition ships.
    • Only use tech 1 and tech 2 modules that you need. Compare variations to find the optimal choice.
  • The angle your camera is facing when you dock will be the same when you undock, despite any ship spinning you may do while docked.
  • Use energy weapons with tech 1 ammo to save ISK by never purchasing ammo.
  • You should stay next to your deployed Mobile Tractor Unit and scoop it once you are done using it. It can easily be destroyed by other players and award a killmail. Players shooting Mobile Tractor Units will only get suspect status.
