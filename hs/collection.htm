Guides.htm/Hearthstone/Collecting All Cards
Cards
  1. Complete specical tasks to get cards, such as playing during expansion releases or on specific days.
Dust
  1. Disenchant extra cards via the "Disenchant Extra Cards" button.
  2. Disenchant Classic cards that have been changed in a recent balance patch, because they can be disenchanted for their full crafting cost.
  3. If you don't have all non-Classic cards, disenchant non-golden extra Classic cards, which are displayed when you search for "extra classic", until you have 2 of that card total (or 1 for legendary cards).
  4. Craft non-golden wild cards, starting with the most useful for adventures, because they cannot be obtained from packs or monthly season endings.
  5. Craft golden versions of cards that cannot be obtained otherwise, such as the Beckoner of Evil.
  6. Craft golden wild cards.
  7. Save remaining dust for future card releases or craft golden standard cards.
Gold
  1. Purchase the first tier of every Solo Adventure available, starting with the oldest. HIGHEST PRIORITY because wild adventures cannot be purchased.
  2. Unlock and complete Normal and Class Challenges on all Solo Adventure tiers.
  3. If you need cards from packs that aren't offered as Arena rewards, buy specific card packs.
  4. Try to get as many wins as possible in the Arena.
  5. Win three games against other players as many times as possible to gain 10 gold each time.
Packs
  1. Win Tavern Brawl at least once every week.
  2. Complete specical tasks to get packs, such as playing during expansion releases or on specific days.
Quests
  1. You shouldn't end a day with three quests remaining. Complete quests until you have two or less.
Ranked
  1. Get at least rank 20 every monthly season.
  2. Improve your rank as much as possible, as every additional rank improves your reward at the end of the season.
