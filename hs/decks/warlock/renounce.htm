iterami/Guides.htm/Hearthstone/Renounce Darkness Warlock
Playing:
  • Hard mulligan for Reounce Darkness.
  • The coin should not be used for additional card draw, since it becomes a lot better after Reounce Darkness is used. You can use the coin to reduce your hand size from 10 to 9 at the end of your turn, since the mana cost of the Warlock card you draw will be reduced by 1 anyway.
Deck Creation:
  • Add 2 Renounce Darkness to your deck. Since Renounce Darkness replaces Warlock cards in your hand and deck, you should fill the rest of your deck with Warlock cards.
  • You should prioritize adding Warlock cards that add other Warlock cards to your hand without damaging your hero. Make sure you always select the best Warlock card:
    • Call of the Void
      Common Warlock Classic
    • Dark Peddler
      Common Warlock LoE
    • Dark Possession
      Rare Warlock WW
    • Demonic Project
      Common Warlock TBP
    • Kabal Trafficker
      Epic Warlock MSoG
    • Rin, the First Disciple
      Legendary Warlock K&C
  • Next prioritize adding Warlock cards that help you get closer to drawing Renounce Darkness by drawing cards:
    • Kobold Librarian
      Common Warlock K&C
    • Mortal Coil
      Warlock Basic
    • Sense Demons
      Common Warlock Classic
    • The Soularium
      Legendary Warlock TBP
  • Next prioritize adding Warlock cards that help you clear/control the board:
    • Abyssal Enforcer
      Common Warlock MSoG
    • Bloodreaver Gul'dan
      Legendary Warlock KotFT
    • Corrupting Mist
      Rare Warlock KotFT
    • Defile
      Rare Warlock KotFT
    • Demonwrath
      Rare Warlock BRM
    • Despicable Dreadlord
      Rare Warlock BRM
    • DOOM!
      Epic Warlock WotOG
    • Dread Infernal
      Warlock Basic
    • Dreadsteed
      Epic Warlock TGT
    • Felfire Potion
      Rare Warlock MSoG
    • Hellfire
      Warlock Basic
    • Hooked Reaver
      Rare Warlock K&C
    • Shadowflame
      Rare Warlock Classic
    • Siphon Soul
      Rare Warlock Classic
    • Twisting Nether
      Epic Warlock Classic
    • Voidlord
      Epic Warlock K&C
  • You can add Prince Malchezaar to your deck, since he replaces 1 randomized card with 6 unreplaced legendary minions.
