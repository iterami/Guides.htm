iterami/Guides.htm/Prey (2006)
  • Open the console with "Ctrl + Alt + ~".
  • If you want to continue using commands such as god and noclip, you must enter them after each load.
Useful Commands Notes
giveammo Refills your health and ammo.
give all Gives you every weapon available and refills your health and ammo. Some portions of the game may behave strangely because they assume you do not have specific weapons.
god Makes you immune to all forms of damage.
kill Kills yourself.
noclip Makes your character fly and pass through all solid objects. Some portions of the game may behave strangely if you pass through triggers in a different order.
notarget Makes you invisible to enemies.
