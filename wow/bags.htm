Bag Progression:
  1. Any bags you find during leveling.
  2. 12 slot [Huge Brown Sack], purchased in capital cities for 10 gold. Price reduced by reputation.
  3. 16 slot bags purchased from Alliance/Horde faction quartermasters, which require revered standing. Can be found next to capital city flight paths.
Bag Contents:
  • [Darkmoon Adventerer's Guide]
  • Fishing Pole:
    1. [Fishing Pole]: sold by fishing supplies vendors.
    2. [Strong Fishing Pole]: sold by fishing supplies vendors.
    3. [Bone Fishing Pole]: reward from fishing daily quests.
