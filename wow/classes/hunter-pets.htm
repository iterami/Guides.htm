  • Cunning pets give you and your primary pet +8% movement speed.
  • Ferocity pets give you and your primary pet +10% leech.
  • Tenacity pets give you and your primary pet +5% maximum health and gives you Survival of the Fittest, which reduces damage taken by you and your pet by 20% for 6 seconds with a 3 minute cooldown.
  • All pets know:
    • Basic Attack: damaging ability that uses focus.
    • Dash: increase movement speed by 80% for 10 seconds, 20 second cooldown.
    • Growl: generates threat and taunts the enemy target.
