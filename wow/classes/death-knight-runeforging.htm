Runeforge Notes
Rune of Razorice
  • attacks deal extra frost damage
  • +3% damage dealt by your frost attacks to target, stacking up to 5 times
Rune of the Fallen Crusader your attacks have chance to heal you for 6% of maximum health and +15% strength for 15 seconds
Rune of the Stoneskin Gargoyle +5% armor and +5% all stats
