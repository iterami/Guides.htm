Improving the Default UI:
  • Chat Settings:
    • Combat Log:
      • Increase background opacity to ~75%
      • Rename to "L"
      • Set "Font Size" to "12 pt"
    • General:
      • Increase background opacity to ~75%
      • Rename to "G"
      • Set "Font Size" to "12 pt"
      • Unlock the window and increase size
  • Interface Settings:
    • ActionBars:
      • Check "Bottom Right Bar"
      • Check "Right Bar"
      • Check "Right Bar 2"
    • Buffs and Debuffs:
      • Check "Show All Enemy Debuffs"
      • Uncheck "Consolidate Buffs"
    • Controls:
      • Check "Auto Loot".
    • Raid Profiles:
      • Change "Display Health Text" to "Health Remaining"
      • Check "Display Class Colors"
      • Check "Display Pets"
      • Check "Display Power Bars"
      • Check "Use Raid-Style Party Frames"
      • Uncheck "Display Border"
Preparing your Character:
  1. Travel to the nearest capital city.
    1. Set your Hearthstone to return you to this capital city.
    2. Go to the bank and create every heirloom item that your character is capable of using. Equip the heirlooms relevant to your planned specialization and deposit the rest. Make sure you don't leave any heirloom-compatible slots empty.
