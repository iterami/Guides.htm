iterami/Guides.htm/World of Warcraft/Professions/Cooking
  • Every character can and should learn cooking.
  • Complete 1 cooking daily quest each day.
  • Pandaren get +15 cooking and +100% Well Fed bonuses as a racial bonus.
  • You can cook with materials currently stored in your bank.
  • Training:
    1. 1-40: [Spice Bread]
    2. 41-50: [Brilliant Smallfish]
    3. 51-100: [Longjaw Mud Snapper]
