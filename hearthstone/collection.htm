iterami/Guides.htm/Hearthstone/Collecting All Cards
Cards
  1. Complete specical tasks to get cards, such as playing during expansion releases or on specific days.
Dust
  1. Disenchant extra cards via the "Disenchant Extra Cards" button.
  2. Craft non-golden wild cards starting with the most useful, because these cannot be obtained from packs that cost gold or monthly season endings. Neutral cards tend to be included in more decks than class cards.
  3. Craft golden versions of cards that cannot be obtained otherwise, such as the Beckoner of Evil.
  4. Craft golden wild cards starting with your favorites.
  5. Save remaining dust for crafting cards recently moved to wild, or craft golden standard cards.
Gold
  1. Purchase the first tier of every Solo Adventure available for purchase with gold, starting with the oldest. HIGHEST PRIORITY because wild adventures cannot be purchased with gold.
  2. Unlock and complete Normal and Class Challenges on all Solo Adventure tiers.
  3. If you need cards from packs that aren't offered as Arena rewards, buy specific card packs with gold.
  4. Try to get as many wins as possible in the Arena.
  5. Win 3 games against other players as many times as possible to gain 10 gold each time, up to 100 gold for 30 wins per day.
Packs
  1. Win Tavern Brawl at least once every week. Depending on how much gold you have, you might not have to skip Heroic Tavern Brawl and other paid brawls.
  2. Complete specical tasks to get packs, such as picking your tournament champions, playing during expansion releases, and playing on specific days.
Quests
  1. Reroll 50 gold quests, then difficult 60+ gold quests.
  2. You shouldn't end a day with 3 quests remaining. Complete quests until you have 2 or less.
  3. If possible, avoid completing 50 gold quests that you can just reroll another day.
Ranked
  1. Win at least 5 standard or wild ranked games every monthly season.
  2. Get at least rank 20 in standard or wild every monthly season.
  3. Improve your rank as much as possible, as every additional rank improves your reward at the end of the season. Rewards greatly improve at rank 15, 10, 5, and legend.
