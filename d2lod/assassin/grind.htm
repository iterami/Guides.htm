iterami/Guides.htm/Diablo 2: Lord of Destruction/Grind Assassin
  • Download: GrindAssassin.d2s (~1KB)
  • Play an Assassin that gets a penalty to various stats:
    • -99% Better Chance of Getting Magic Items
    • -99% Extra Gold from Monsters
    • -99% to Experience Gained
  • Rules:
    • You must keep the two "GrindAssassin's Small Charm" in your inventory at all times.
    • You cannot use experience shrines.
