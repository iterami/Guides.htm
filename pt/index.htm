iterami/Guides.htm/Planescape: Torment
  • Farming:
    • Quickloot is the best way to loot items from fallen enemies.
    • Respawning enemies can be farmed to gain unlimited experience, purchase all limited-stock items, and purchase unlimited amounts of unlimited stock items.
    • Use tattoos that increase Wisdom to increase experience gained when farming.
  • Inventory:
    • Always carry at least one 99 stack of every stackable unlimited item.
    • Always carry one 99 stack of Heart Charms on every character.
    • Always carry tattoos that increase ability scores, just in case you need an extra boost before certain encounters.
  • Only one party member needs to reach a loading zone for all party members to move to a new area.
  • The Nameless One's recommendations:
    • Ability Score Minimums:
      • Charisma: 13
      • Constitution: 9
      • Dexterity: 13
      • Intelligence: 16
      • Strength: 9
      • Wisdom: 18
    • Class: Mage
