iterami/Guides.htm/Planescape: Torment
  • Always carry in inventory:
    • One 99 stack of every stackable unlimited item.
    • One 99 stack of Heart Charms on every character.
    • One Iron Prybar which is needed for some quest actions.
    • One stack of Junk in case you need to enter or exit the Trash Warrens.
    • Tattoos that increase ability scores, just in case you need an extra boost before certain encounters.
    • Combat:
      • Do all actions, such as clicking on an enemy to attack or casting a spell, while the game is paused.
    • Farming:
      • Quickloot is the best way to loot items from fallen enemies.
      • Respawning enemies can be farmed to gain unlimited experience, purchase all limited-stock items, and purchase unlimited amounts of unlimited stock items.
      • Use tattoos that increase Wisdom to increase experience gained when farming.
  • Only one party member needs to reach a loading zone for all party members to move to a new area.
  • The Nameless One's recommendations:
    • Ability Score Minimums:
      • Charisma: 13
      • Constitution: 9
      • Dexterity: 13
      • Intelligence: 16
      • Strength: 9
      • Wisdom: 18
    • Class: Mage
    • Faction: Dustmen
  • Shops:
    • Some limited-stock items are restocked after you rest, allowing unlimited purchases.
