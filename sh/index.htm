  • The Island Castle:
    • Baking bread is your best option for gathering food.
    • Build a marketplace and all hovels on portions of the map that are not accessible by your citizens.
    • To get rid of all trees on the island:
      1. Chop down all the trees that your wood cutters can reach.
      2. Keep at least 1 wood camp to chop down any new trees that begin to grow.
      3. Wait until all unreacheable trees have died.
    • Use your lord to kill all rabbits on the island.
