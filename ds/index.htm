Guides.htm/Dungeon Siege 2
  • Always recite a chant when you reach an Incantation Shrine.
  • Be careful when changing weapons or spells in your spellbook, for your character will attack with the most recently changed weapon/spell unless you manually change back to which attack you prefer.
  • If you cannot pick up an item automatically, try picking it up with a click or when your inventory is empty.
  • If you don't have your zoomed-out minimap open and don't check your map, the map won't get revealed.
  • If you need health or mana but don't have any potions, park your characters in a safe area and take a break to stretch your legs. Make sure your game isn't paused.
  • Spells that instantly hit an enemy without travel time can be fired through walls.
  • Statue Guardian: Azunai, Xeria, Elandir, Zaramoth. Get it wrong five times for additional boss monsters.
  • Teleporters with guaranteed boss monsters near them:
    1. Southern Greilyn Jungle
  • The Armorer's Apprentice: #3.
