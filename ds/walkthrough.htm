iterami/Guides.htm/Dungeon Siege 2/Walkthrough
  1. Choose a build and create your character.
Chapter 1: The Siege of Greilyn Beach:
  1. Complete The Siege of Greilyn Beach:
    • Equip and use only weapons or spells that train your character build specialty.
    • Make sure all of your settings are correct.
    • Don't save items or potions, for all your equipment will be lost at the end of the seige.
    • Use your powers on the Ketril.
Chapter 2: Prisoner of War
  1. Talk with both Mercenary Prisoners.
  2. Use the Cell Door to begin next cutscene.
  3. Equip the weapons/spells that train your character build specialty.
  4. Open the Cell Door on the opposite side and talk to the two Mercenary Prisoners.
  5. Take the lift to the Blacksmith Shop.
  6. Talk to Apprentice Telinu, spam 1.
  7. Talk to Apprentice Telinu again, say 1311. Equip the armor you receive.
  8. Sell everything you have not equipped to Weaponsmith Duma.
  9. Talk to Enchantress Lumilla to get another quest.
  10. Activate the Town of Eirulan teleporter.
  11. Talk to Necromancer Eranith to unlock more handbook entries.
  12. Take the lift to the Inn.
  13. Open the Inn Door.
  14. Go upstairs and talk to Inkeeper Leni to unlock another handbook entry.
  15. Open all three doors upstairs.
  16. Go downstairs and open the door behind the bar.
  17. Pick up the Hak'u book on the table.
  18. Talk to the Grizzled Bar Patron and spam 1 to get a quest.
  19. Open the two doors at the far end of the inn.
  20. Take the lift to the Blacksmith Shop.
  21. Open the Eirulan North Gate and take the lift down to the jungle.
