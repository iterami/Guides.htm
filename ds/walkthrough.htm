# Before you Start Notes
1 Choose a build and create your character.
# Act 1 Chapter 1: The Siege of Greilyn Beach Notes
2 Complete The Siege of Greilyn Beach
  • Equip and use only weapons or spells that train your character build specialty.
  • Make sure all of your settings are correct.
  • Don't save items or potions, for all your equipment will be lost at the end of the seige.
  • Use your powers on the Ketril.
# Act 1 Chapter 2: Prisoner of War Notes
3 Talk with both Mercenary Prisoners.
4 Use the Cell Door to begin next cutscene.
5 Equip the weapons/spells that train your character build specialty.
6 Open the Cell Door on the opposite side and talk to the two Mercenary Prisoners.
7 Take the lift to the Blacksmith Shop.
8 Talk to Apprentice Telinu. Spam 1.
9 Talk to Apprentice Telinu again. Equip the armor you receive. 1311
10 Sell everything you have not equipped to Weaponsmith Duma.
11 Talk to Enchantress Lumilla to get another quest.
12 Activate the Town of Eirulan teleporter.
13 Talk to Necromancer Eranith to unlock more handbook entries.
14 Take the lift to the Inn.
15 Open the Inn Door.
16 Go upstairs and talk to Inkeeper Leni to unlock another handbook entry.
17 Open all three doors upstairs.
18 Go downstairs and open the door behind the bar.
19 Pick up the Hak'u book on the table.
20 Talk to the Grizzled Bar Patron. Spam 1.
21 Open the two doors at the far end of the inn.
22 Take the lift to the Blacksmith Shop.
23 Open the Eirulan North Gate and take the lift down to the jungle.
24 Talk to Lirrit to unlock another handbook entry.
25 Talk to Esselte to get Lesser Chant of Fortification. Recite the chant at the incantation shrine.
26 Travel east to activate the next cutscene. Defeat the enemies.
27 Open the Morden Stockade Gate.
# Act 1 Chapter 3: The Morden Towers Notes
28 Open the basket and the Dryad Outpost Gate.
29 Destroy two barrels in the camp right after the bridge.
30 Travel west at the next fork. Activate the statue to receive guaranteed set item drop.
31 Travel west again before you reach the Morden gate for another statue to activate.
32 Do not talk to Morden-Viir Thug Leader Grekney until you have defeated all of the Hak'u and opened all barrels and baskets.
33 Morden-Viir Thug Leader Grekney, select option #2, and then kill all the Morden before going through the gate.
34 Take the western path after the gate for additional enemies, barrels, and a chest to open.
35 Destroy the Morden tower.
36 Head left of the path to destroy some barrels and open a basket.
37 Destroy all barrels, open all baskets, collect all chants, and return to Eirulan with the newly activated teleporter.
38 Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
