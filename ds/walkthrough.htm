# Before you Start Notes
1 Choose a build and create your character. Commit to only one build per character.
# Act 1 Chapter 1: The Siege of Greilyn Beach Notes
2 Complete The Siege of Greilyn Beach
  1. Make sure all of your settings are correct.
  2. Increase the size of your minimap.
  3. Equip and use only weapons/spells that train your character build specialty.
  4. Don't save items or potions, for all your equipment will be lost at the end of the seige.
  5. Use your powers on the Ketril.
# Act 1 Chapter 2: Prisoner of War Notes
3 Talk with both Mercenary Prisoners.
4 Use the Cell Door to begin next cutscene.
5 Equip the weapons/spells that train your character build specialty.
6 Open the Cell Door on the opposite side and talk to the two Mercenary Prisoners.
7 Take the lift to the Blacksmith Shop.
8 Talk to Apprentice Telinu. Spam 1.
9 Talk to Apprentice Telinu again. Equip the armor you receive. 1311
10 Sell everything you have not equipped to Weaponsmith Duma.
11 Talk to Enchantress Lumilla to get another quest. 2111.
12 Activate the Town of Eirulan teleporter.
13 Talk to Necromancer Eranith to unlock more handbook entries.
14 Take the lift to the Inn.
15 Open the Inn Door.
16 Go upstairs and talk to Inkeeper Leni to unlock another handbook entry.
17 Open all three doors upstairs.
18 Go downstairs and open the door behind the bar.
19 Pick up the Hak'u book on the table.
20 Talk to the Grizzled Bar Patron. Spam 1.
21 Open the two doors at the far end of the inn.
22 Take the lift to the Blacksmith Shop.
23 Open the Eirulan North Gate and take the lift down to the jungle.
24 Talk to Lirrit to unlock another handbook entry.
25 Talk to Esselte to get Lesser Chant of Fortification. Recite the chant at the incantation shrine.
26 Travel east to activate the next cutscene. Defeat the enemies.
27 Open the Morden Stockade Gate. Spam 1.
# Act 1 Chapter 3: The Morden Towers Notes
28 Open the basket and the Dryad Outpost Gate.
29 Destroy two barrels in the camp right after the bridge.
30 Travel west at the next fork. Activate the statue to receive guaranteed set item drop.
31 Travel west again before you reach the Morden gate for another statue to activate.
32 Do not talk to Morden-Viir Thug Leader Grekney until you have defeated all of the Hak'u and opened all barrels and baskets.
33 Talk to Morden-Viir Thug Leader Grekney, select option #2, and then kill all the Morden before going through the gate.
34 Take the western path after the gate for additional enemies, barrels, and a chest to open.
35 Follow the instructions to destroy the Morden tower. Spam 1.
36 Head left of the path to destroy some barrels and open a basket.
37 Destroy all barrels, open all baskets, collect all chants, and return to Eirulan with the newly activated teleporter.
38 Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
39 Return to the Northern Greilyn Jungle and continue on the path up heading west.
40 Destroy the three barrels behind the tower and open the chest within the tower.
41 After the cutscene, cross the bridge to the north. Kill all enemies and destroy all barrels.
42 Return to the path and talk to Morain. Spam 1.
43 Recite a chant at the incantation shrine.
44 Destroy the Morden tower. Open the basket and destroy the barrels while you wait for it to explode.
45 Destroy the barrels and open the chest within the Hak'u camp.
46 Open the basket to the north, then loot everything within the cave behind the waterfall.
47 Before you destroy the Morden tower, open all baskets and destroy all barrels.
48 Destroy the Morden tower. Destroy the barrels directly to the south after you pass through the ruins.
49 Go down the hill to the east. Open the basket and destroy the barrels.
50 Once you enter the Western Greilyn Jungle, open the basket and destroy five barrels to the east.
51 Travel west to trigger a cutscene. Before you enter the cave, destroy three barrels behind it.
52 Once inside the cave, trigger a cutscene in the northwest corner.
53 Open all baskets and chests and destroy all barrels.
54 Activate the lever to extend the bridge.
55 Pick up the Tattered Diary.
56 Travel south along the path that leads out of the cave, destroying barrels along the way.
57 Go to the tower north of the incantation shrine. Get the Lesser Chant of Dexterity and open the door that matches your specialty.
58 Return to Eirulan. Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
59 Return back to the incantation shrine and recite the chant that increases your primary stat.
60 Go up the fallen rock bridge to the north west and open the chest behind the cave entrance.
61 Open all baskets/chests and destroy all barrels. Recite the chant that increases your primary stat at the incantation shrine.
62 Cross the artificial bridge, exit the cave, and continue on the path heading south. Open one basket and destroy three barrels.
63 Open the basket in the Hak'u camp.
64 Recite the chant that increases your primary stat at the incantation shrine.
65 Open the basket directly south of the incantation shrine.
66 Open the basket, destroy the barrels, and destroy the Morden tower.
67 Activate the Western Greilyn teleporter, but head west. Open the Magic Bookcase, get the Lesser Chant of Mage Health, and recite the chant that increases your primary stat at the incantation shrine.
68 Destroy the two barrels southwest of the teleporter, then head west to open a basket, destroy two barrels, and get the Lesser Chant of Casting.
69 Enter the western hole in the wall. Get the Lesser Chant of Ranger Health, destroy four barrels, open one basket, and open one chest.
70 Return to Eirulan. Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
71 Take the lift to the Prison. Talk to Prisoner Warden Celia. Spam 1.
72 Take the lift to the Blacksmith Shop, open the Door, and then take the lifts to the southeast to the Great Hall.
73 Open the Infirmary Door and talk to Hesla. Spam 1.
74 Open the Great Hall Door. Talk to Tellendril for another handbook entry, pick up the two books and talk to Taar. Spam 1.
# Act 1 Chapter 4: The Plague Notes
75 Open the door to the south of the Great Hall.
76 Take the lift to the Pet Shop.
77 Open the Eastern Pet Shop door, talk to Pet Merchant Neda, and open the Northern Pet Shop door. 41111
78 Open the Door to the southwest and talk to Lelani. Pick up the Child's Doll. Spam 1.
79 Go east up the ramp, open the Door, and talk to Leanne. Spam 1.
80 Take the lift to the Great Hall and speak to Rokhar the Mage. Spam 1.
81 Take the lift to the Inn and then take the lift to the Falls.
82 Open the Door to the east and talk to Fenella. Pick up the Flask of Elven Ale. Open the other Door.
83 Travel along the falls path until you reach the Door. Open it and talk to Tamari. Spam 1.
84 Activate the Lever and return to the Blacksmith Shop. Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
85 Return to the Western Greilyn Jungle and open the Outpost Gate to your south. Spam 1.
86 Use the armor stand, break three barrels, open one basket, and get the Lesser Chant of Fighter Health. Leave the outpost and close the Gate behind you.
87 Destroy three barrels southeast of the first hill. Destroy one barrel on the left side of the path after the stone "bridge". Destroy six barrels and open two basket behind the entrance to the Elven Shrine. Enter the Elven Shrine.
88 Destroy three barrels and open two baskets. Open the Door and then open either Door at the end of the hallway. Destroy two barrels and activate the lever.
89 Activate the button on the western wall. Get a weapon from the weapon barrel and open two baskets. Pick up one Green Life Cube and put it into the socket. Activate the lever.
90 Open the basket. Pick up two Yellow Sight Cubes. Activate the button. Pick up three Red Blindness Cubes. Place a yellow cube into the socket. Activate the lever.
91 Open the chest. Activate the button. Pick up two Purple Death Cubes. Place a red cube into the socket. Place a purple cube into the socket. Place a yellow cube into the socket. Place a red cube into the socket. Place a purple cube into the socket. Activate the lever.
92 Get the Chants of Fighter/Mage/Ranger Power, open the basket, and open the bookcase. Open the southern Door.
93 Open the eastern Door. Destroy three barrels and open one chest. Open the southern Door.
94 Open the two eastern Doors. Open the southern Door.
95 Open the eastern Door. Open two baskets and one bookcase. Destroy two barrels on the sides of the southern staircase. Open the southern door.
96 Destroy one barrel and open one armor stand. Open the southern Door.
97 Get the Lesser Chant of Prosperity west of the door you came in through. Complete the quest and go through the eastern door.
98 Activate the Elven Shrine teleporter. Return to Eirulan.
99 Take the lifts to the Great Hall and talk to Taar. She will join your party if you spam 1.
# Act 1 Chapter 5: The Dryad Exile Colony Notes
100 Take the lift to the Pet Shop and talk to Laenne in the room above it.
101 Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
102 Leave Eirulan through the South Gate. Destroy six barrels and open one basket in the Hak'u camps.
103 Continue south. Destroy three barrels and pick up the Nettle Cluster. Explore the cave.
104 Continue on the path up the hill. Destroy six barrels and open one basket at the height of the Hak'u camp.
105 Destroy three barrels and open a chest in the Hak'u camp behind the stone pillar.
106 Destroy two barrels and open one chest before entering the underground sections.
107 Enter the dungeon area. Open two baskets and use the Chalice. Open two more baskets and use the bookcase.
108 Destroy three barrels before entering the natural cave. Open one basket and destroy two barrels inside.
109 Destroy six barrels and open one chest by the ruins north of the path. Use the button on the inner wall for an additional basket.
110 Activate the Southern Greilyn Jungle teleporter. Destroy five barrels. Return to Eirulan.
111 Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
112 Return to the Southern Greilyn Jungle. Destroy one barrel and open one chest within the pit. Destroy two barrels north of the pit. Destroy two barrels south of the pit.
113 OPTIONAL STEP: Use the lever at the bottom of the pit. Open the door. Use the armor stand and weapon barrel. This step is optional because it is included in the farming run.
114 Destroy four barrels and open one basket in the Hak'u camp to the northeast. Head north into the cave.
115 Open the chest. Destroy seven barrels and open two baskets. Continue through the cave.
116 Enter Rokhar's Rift Site. Open three baskets, open a chest, and destroy three barrels. Recover your health/mana and then talk to Rokhar. Defeat the wolves and talk to him again. Return back through the cave. Spam 1.
117 Enter the cave to the south. Open the chest.
118 Head north up the thin section, past the mushrooms. Open the chest and destroy two barrels.
119 Destroy two barrels and open one basket in the first Hak'u camp. Destroy one barrel and open two baskets in the second Hak'u camp.
120 Destroy three barrels by the tower. Open the Door. There are dangerous monsters below.
121 Rescue the Azunite Scholar in the Dryad Exile Colony. Open one basket, two weapon barrels, and one chest, and destroy seven barrels. Talk to the Azunite Scholar twice. Spam 1.
# Act 1 Chapter 6: Leaving Greilyn Isle Notes
122 Head southwest. Pick up the Nettle Cluster by the cave entrance. Enter the cave and talk to Hrawn the Hak'u. Exit the cave and recite the Lesser Chant of Prosperity. Spam 1.
123 Continue on the path heading east. Destroy one barrel and open two baskets in the Hak'u camp. Open the tower Door. Destroy three barrels and open one basket inside it. Activate the Eastern Greilyn Jungle teleporter. Return to Eirulan.
124 Return to the Pet Shop to complete the Dire Wolf quest. Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
125 Return to the Eastern Greilyn Jungle. Continue east on the path. Destroy four barrels and open one chest in the camp.
126 Continue across the bridge for now. Destroy two barrels in the Hak'u camp. Enter the cave.
127 Destroy 11 barrels and open one basket in the eastern portion of the cave. Destroy six barrels in the next cave nook. Destroy two barrels behind some stalagmites before the path goes up a ramp. Exit the cave.
128 Open the Door to the building. Get the Lesser Chant of Magic Awareness, get the Lesser Chant of Ranged Awareness, get the armor from the armor stand, and open one basket. Pick up the book "The Skath". Talk to Vix. He will join your party if you spam 1.
129 Enter the cave. Destroy three barrels by the sunlight pit. Go down the ramp and destroy four more barrels. Destroy six more barrels after you pass the big Hak'u camp. Destroy five barrels and open one chest in the next Hak'u camp. Destroy two more barrels. Destroy five more barrels in the next Hak'u camp and three more barrels in the center of this big room.
130 Head down through the small cave entrance to the west. Destroy six barrels, open one chest, pick up a Nettle Cluster, and open the Door. Return to the main cave.
131 Exit the cave. Activate the Kithraya Valley teleporter. Return to Eirulan.
132 Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
133 Return to the Kithraya Valley. Destroy three barrels before going downhill. Destroy four barrels behind the mana bush. Enter the cave.
134 Destroy seven egg sacks in the first side area. Destroy three more egg sacks after you go downhill. Destroy two more egg sacks in the room with the toxic gas.
135 Kill the Plagued Maltratar Overlord to open a hidden area to the north. Destroy 18 egg sacks inside.
136 Head north after viewing the posession cutscene. Destroy six egg sacks. Destroy three egg sacks in the next room with toxic gas. Continue northeast out of the cave.
137 Destroy the Shard and pick up the Nettle Cluster. Do not recite a chant at the shrine. Talk to the Azunite Scholar. Use the portal. Activate the Azunite Desert Teleporter to the west. Spam 1.
138 Enter the camp and talk to Captain Suzor. Go inside the building and talk to Soldier Balamar. Use the Weapon Barrel and the Magic Bookcase. Spam 1.
138 Return to Eirulan. Give the four Nettle Clusters to Lumilla.
139 Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
