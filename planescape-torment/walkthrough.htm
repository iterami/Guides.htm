iterami/Guides.htm/Planescape: Torment/Walkthrough
This walkthrough assumes:
  • When leveling up and gaining ability score points, you will only improve skills as much as the ability score table allows you to add during level up.
  • You will be playing a mage.
  • You will choose every dialogue option at least once, unless otherwise noted.
  • You will fully physically explore every area in order to reveal the map.
  • You will join the Dustman faction.
  • You will only kill enemies that respawn or are required for quest completion.
  1. Character Creation
  2. Mortuary
  3. Sigil
# Character Creation Notes
1
  • Make sure your settings are correct.
  • Optimal ability scores:
    • Charisma: 13
    • Constitution: 9
    • Dexterity: 9
    • Intelligence: 17
    • Strength: 9
    • Wisdom: 18
# Mortuary Notes
2 Morte will speak to you. Take the scalpel from the container by the western door. Kill the Zombie Worker that is holding the Preparation Room Key and pick it up. Don't use Morte to fight the zombie, as his health does not automatically regenerate.
3 Open the door located at the western end of the room. Morte will speak to you again after you pass through.
4 Open the door at the northern end of the room. Open the next door at the northern end of the room. Open the next door at the northeastern end of the room. Open the next door at the eastern end of the room. Open the next door at the southern end of the room. Go up the stairs to the third floor. You will return to the Mortuary later on, so you should not loot containers or speak to any NPCs.
5 Select control of only Morte. Head south along the wall until you get to the shelves next to the locked door in the southwest of the room. Pick up the key and open the locked door. Go down the stairs back to the second floor. The dustmen are not able to speak directly to Morte, so you will be safe from getting caught.
6 Go down the stairs to the first floor. Speak to Soego. Tell him you are lost, then ask him to lead you out. He will unlock the door and let you leave the Mortuary. If the Dustman Guards speak to you, ask them if there are any guides around that can direct visitors, then thank them and they will leave you alone.
# Sigil Notes
7 Travel west to the northwestern portion of the Hive, then travel north to Ragpicker's Square. Enter the midwife's hut and speak to Old Mebbeth. Say that you have some questions for her. Ask her if she is a witch and ask her to teach you magic. She will give you a Black-Barbed Seed and send you to the market.
8 Exit the midwife's hut and travel east to the northwestern portion of the Hive. Travel southwest to the southwestern portion of the Hive. Speak to the Hive Merchant that mentions fruits, herbs, and spices.
9 Travel southeast to the southeastern portion of the Hive. Speak to Mourns-for-Trees, located directly east of the exit leading to the northeastern portion of the Hive. Return to Old Mebbeth. Accept your level up before continuing with the quest.
10 Return to the market in the southwestern portion of the Hive and speak to Giscorl, located near the previous Hive Merchant you spoke to. After you get Mebbeth's Wash, return to Old Mebbeth.
11 Return to the market again and speak to Kossah-Jai, located near the previous 2 merchants you spoke to, then speak to Meir'am, located to the south. Then return to the merchants and speak to the Hive Merchant that mentions cups, plates, and tankards. Return to Meir'am with the tankard and fill it with ink. Return to Old Mebbeth and become a mage. Once you have become a mage:
  1. Accept your level up.
  2. Equip your earrings.
  3. Copy the 3 scrolls into your spell book.
12 Travel to the southeastern portion of the Hive and into the Smoldering Corpse Bar. Speak to Dak'kon and he will join your party. Speak to Dak'kon again and ask to learn the teachings of Zerthimon. Alternate using the Unbroken Circle of Zerthimon in Dak'kon's inventory and speaking to Dak'kon.
  • The correct answer to Dak'kon's questions about the Unbroken Circle of Zerthimon is always the first choice.
  • You will need 19 wisdom to complete all circles. Grind up 300 Copper Commons by killing respawning Hive Thugs and selling their loot. Purchase a Tattoo of the Spirit from Fell's Studio east of the Smoldering Corpse Bar.
