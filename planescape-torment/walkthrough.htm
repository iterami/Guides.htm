iterami/Guides.htm/Planescape: Torment/Walkthrough
This walkthrough assumes:
  • When leveling up and gaining ability score points, you will only improve skills as much as the ability score table allows you to add during level up.
  • You will be a lawful good mage that joins the Dustman faction.
  • You will choose every dialogue option at least once, unless otherwise noted.
  • You will fully physically explore every area in order to reveal the map.
  • You will only kill enemies that respawn or are required for quest completion.
  • You will store items that you don't need in a safe place, such as the Gathering Dust Bar.
  • Your party will ultimately consist of:
    1. The Nameless One
    2. Morte
    3. Dak'kon
    4. Annah
# Step Notes
1
  • Make sure your settings are correct.
  • Optimal ability scores:
    • Charisma: 13
    • Constitution: 9
    • Dexterity: 9
    • Intelligence: 17
    • Strength: 9
    • Wisdom: 18
2 Morte will speak to you. Take the scalpel from the container by the western door. Kill the Zombie Worker that is holding the Preparation Room Key and pick it up. Don't use Morte to fight the zombie, as his health does not automatically regenerate.
3 Open the door located at the western end of the room. Morte will speak to you again after you pass through.
4 Open the door at the northern end of the room. Open the next door at the northern end of the room. Open the next door at the northeastern end of the room. Open the next door at the eastern end of the room. Open the next door at the southern end of the room. Go up the stairs to the third floor. You will return to the Mortuary later on, so you should not loot containers or speak to any NPCs.
5 Select control of only Morte. Head south along the wall until you get to the shelves next to the locked door in the southwest of the room. Pick up the key and open the locked door. Go down the stairs back to the second floor. The dustmen are not able to speak directly to Morte, so you will be safe from getting caught.
6 Go down the stairs to the first floor. Speak to Soego. Tell him you are lost, then ask him to lead you out. He will unlock the door and let you leave the Mortuary. If the Dustman Guards speak to you, ask them if there are any guides around that can direct visitors, then thank them and they will leave you alone.
7 Travel west to the northwestern portion of the Hive, then travel north to Ragpicker's Square. Enter the midwife's hut and speak to Old Mebbeth. Say that you have some questions for her. Ask her if she is a witch and ask her to teach you magic. She will give you a Black-Barbed Seed and send you to the market.
8 Exit the midwife's hut and travel east to the northwestern portion of the Hive. Travel southwest to the southwestern portion of the Hive. Speak to the Hive Merchant that mentions fruits, herbs, and spices.
9 Travel southeast to the southeastern portion of the Hive. Speak to Mourns-for-Trees, located directly east of the exit leading to the northeastern portion of the Hive. Return to Old Mebbeth. Accept your level up before continuing with the quest.
10 Return to the market in the southwestern portion of the Hive and speak to Giscorl, located near the previous Hive Merchant you spoke to. After you get Mebbeth's Wash, return to Old Mebbeth.
11 Return to the market again and speak to Kossah-Jai, located near the previous 2 merchants you spoke to, then speak to Meir'am, located to the south. Then return to the merchants and speak to the Hive Merchant that mentions cups, plates, and tankards. Return to Meir'am with the tankard and fill it with ink. Return to Old Mebbeth and become a mage. Once you have become a mage:
  1. Accept your level up.
  2. Equip your earrings.
  3. Copy the 3 scrolls into your spell book.
12 Travel to the southeastern portion of the Hive and into the Smoldering Corpse Bar. Speak to Dak'kon and he will join your party. Speak to Dak'kon again and ask to learn the teachings of Zerthimon. Alternate using the Unbroken Circle of Zerthimon in Dak'kon's inventory and speaking to Dak'kon.
  • The correct answer to Dak'kon's questions about the Unbroken Circle of Zerthimon is always the first choice.
  • You will need 19 wisdom to complete all circles. Grind up 300 Copper Commons by killing respawning Hive Thugs and selling their loot. Purchase a Tattoo of the Spirit from Fell's Studio east of the Smoldering Corpse Bar and equip it to the Nameless One.
  • Use each Circle of Zerthimon in your inventory to add spells to your spell book. Use the 6th, 7th, and 8th Circles of Zerthimon while they are in Dak'kon's inventory.
13 Grind up 900 Copper Commons by killing respawning Hive Thugs and selling their loot. Purchase three Tattoos of Presence from Fell's Studio and equip them to the Nameless One. Equip the Tattoo of the Spirit to Dak'kon for now.
14 Travel to the Office of Vermin and Disease Control, located in the southwestern portion of the Hive. Pick up the Junk located within the northwestern container. Travel to Ragpicker's Square and use the Junk to travel through the portal located at the northern part of the map. Open the door and enter into the Trash Warrens.
15 Anamoli will speak to you. Pick either of the first 2 options, then mention Pharod, then mention the Lady. He will let you pass peacefully. Travel to the southeastern portion of the Trash Warrens, avoiding any detours or fights along the way. Bish will speak to you. Tell him you have no name, assure him you are telling the truth, then mention Pharod. He will let you pass through the trap door into thed Buried Village.
16 Enter Pharod's Court, located at the northeast of the Buried Village. Speak to Pharod. Tell him you've come for information and that you'll help him find the Bronze Sphere in exchange for what he knows. Once you've exhausted every dialogue option, leave the court and exit the Buried Village via the southern exit to the Weeping Stone Catacombs. Barr will let you out through the locked gate. When Pharod agrees to tell you where he gets all his bodies, tell him that he is insulting the craft of magic instead of vowing or lying about not telling anyone.
17 Travel south to the southwestern exit to the Dead Nations Catacombs. Hargrimm the Bleak will speak to you. You must submit. Soego will speak to you. Speak to Hargrimm the Bleak again, then speak to Stale Mary, located in the southwest chamber. She will teach you Stories-Bones-Tell. Ask her to help you speak to the Silent King. She will grant you access to a portal that leads to the Silent King's throne room. Enter it. Hargrimm the Bleak will speak to you. Vow to keep quiet about the Silent King's fate in exchange for being able to leave. Exit the throne room.
18 Exit the Dead Nation to the southwest and enter the Drowned Nations Catacombs. Carefully make your way to the southeast corner by first traveling to the southwest corner and then traveling east. The Bronze Sphere can be found on the dead Dustman's corpse. Return to the Dead Nations Catacombs, then to the Weeping Stone Catacombs, then to Pharod in the Buried Village. Barr will let you back in for free, since you have the Bronze Sphere.
19 Speak to Pharod. Vow to stay your hand and make sure not to threaten him. Once Annah arrives, don't threaten her either. Accept her offer to take you to where she found your body. Once you're there, speak to Annah. She'll give you a Ring of the Traveler and flirt with you. Enter the door Annah mentioned, watch Pharod die, then immediately exit via the way you came in. Return to Pharod's Court and pick up the items that he dropped.
  • Equip the Ring of the Traveler to Annah, as her AC is the worst.
  • Equip Dak'kon's Tattoo of the Spirit to Annah as well, as her wisdom is the worst.
20 Travel to the Gathering Dust Bar, located in the northeastern portion of the Hive. Speak to Emoric about joining the Dustman Faction and he will send you on some quests.
  1. Speak to Norochj, who is also in the Gathering Dust Bar. He'll send you to the southwestern portion of the Hive to speak to Ash-Mantle, located towards the southeast exit. Once you say farewell, he'll try to pick your pocket. Observe his technique and then let him escape. Return to Norochj then return to Emoric.
  2. Speak to Awaiting-Death, who is also in the Gathering Dust Bar. Ask him why he hasn't killed himself yet, then offer to kill him. Release him once he starts to claw at your hands and allow him to keep his money. Return to Emoric.
  3. Speak to Sere the Skeptic, who is also in the Gathering Dust Bar. She is easy to convince. Return to Emoric.
  4. Emoric will ask about Soego, who you previously found in the Dead Nations Catacombs.
  5. Promise that you'll serve the Dustmen and their ideals and you will join the faction.
Avoid mentioning your immortality to Emoric, or he will kick you out of the Dustman faction.
21
  • Purchase various things from various shops before moving on. Farm the respawning enemies in the Alley of Dangerous Angles until you have purchased every item in this step. You may take as long as you want and rest as many times as you want for free in the Open Tomb, located in the northeastern portion of the Hive.
  • Purchase items from the Dustmen Vault, accessed by speaking to Emoric.
    • Bone Dagger. Equip to the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Identify. Teach to Dak'kon.
    • Scroll of Greater Embalming. Teach to Dak'kon and the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Minor Embalming. Teach to Dak'kon and the Nameless One.
  • Purchase all important tattoos from Fell's Studio. Annah will be afraid to speak to Fell, but can be easily calmed down.
    • Tattoo of Action and Tattoo of Greater Action for dexterity. Equip to Annah.
    • Tattoo of Greater Presence for charisma. Equip to the Nameless One.
    • Tattoo of Health for the Nameless One's health regeneration when resting is not an option.
    • Tattoo of Insight and Tattoo of Revelation for intelligence.
    • Tattoo of Might and Tattoo of Greater Might for strength. Equip to Dak'kon.
    • Tattoo of the Spirit and Tattoo of the Soul for wisdom.
    • Tattoo of the Art for the Nameless One.
