iterami/Guides.htm/Planescape: Torment/Walkthrough
This walkthrough assumes:
  • You will be playing a mage.
  • You will choose every dialogue option at least once, unless otherwise noted.
  • You will only kill enemies that enemies that respawn or are required for quest completion.
  1. Character Creation
  2. Mortuary
# Character Creation Notes
1
  • Make sure your settings are correct.
  • Optimal ability scores:
    • Charisma: 13
    • Constitution: 9
    • Dexterity: 9
    • Intelligence: 17
    • Strength: 9
    • Wisdom: 18
# Mortuary Notes
2 Morte will speak to you.
3 Explore and loot the first room.
  • Pick up the 2 Bandages before you pick up the Scalpel.
4 Kill the Zombie Worker that is holing the Preparation Room Key and pick it up.
5 Open the door located at the western end of this room. Morte will speak to you again after you pass through.
6 Speak to the 3 Zombie Workers.
