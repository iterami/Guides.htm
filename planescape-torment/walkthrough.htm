iterami/Guides.htm/Planescape: Torment/Walkthrough
This walkthrough assumes:
  • You will be playing a mage.
  • You will choose every dialogue option at least once, unless otherwise noted.
  • You will only kill enemies that enemies that respawn or are required for quest completion.
  1. Character Creation
  2. Mortuary
# Character Creation Notes
1
  • Make sure your settings are correct.
  • Optimal ability scores:
    • Charisma: 13
    • Constitution: 9
    • Dexterity: 9
    • Intelligence: 17
    • Strength: 9
    • Wisdom: 18
# Mortuary Notes
2 Morte will speak to you. Explore and loot the first room. Kill the Zombie Worker that is holding the Preparation Room Key and pick it up.
3 Open the door located at the western end of the room. Morte will speak to you again after you pass through. Speak to the 3 Zombie Workers.
4 Open the door at the northern end of the room. Explore and loot the room. Speak to each Zombie Worker. Speak to Dhall. Equip the Fist Irons.
5 Open the door located at the northern end of the room. Speak to the Zombie Worker.
6 Open the door located at the north-eastern end of the room. Explore and loot the room. Speak to the Zombie Workers. Speak to Ei-Vene multiple times.
7 Open the door located at the eastern end of the room. Speak to the Zombie Worker. Carefully choose your dialogue options based on how you wish to play your character.
8 Open the door located at the southern end of the room. Explore and loot the room. Bash the locked container.
9 Return to Ei-Vene for a key and a max HP upgrade. Return to the south-east room and go up the stairs.
