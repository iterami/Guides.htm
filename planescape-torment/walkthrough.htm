iterami/Guides.htm/Planescape: Torment/Walkthrough
This walkthrough assumes:
  • When leveling up and gaining ability score points, you will only improve skills as much as the ability score table allows you to add during level up.
  • You will be a lawful good mage that joins the Dustman faction.
  • You will choose every dialogue option at least once, unless otherwise noted.
  • You will fully physically explore every area in order to reveal the map.
  • You will only kill enemies that respawn or are required for quest completion.
  • You will store items that you don't need in a safe place, such as the Gathering Dust Bar.
  • Your party will ultimately consist of:
    1. The Nameless One
    2. Morte
    3. Dak'kon
    4. Annah
# Step Notes
1
  • Make sure your settings are correct.
  • Optimal ability scores:
    • Charisma: 13
    • Constitution: 9
    • Dexterity: 9
    • Intelligence: 17
    • Strength: 9
    • Wisdom: 18
2 Morte will speak to you. Take the scalpel from the container by the western door. Kill the Zombie Worker that is holding the Preparation Room Key and pick it up. Don't use Morte to fight the zombie, as his health does not automatically regenerate.
3 Open the door located at the western end of the room. Morte will speak to you again after you pass through. Open the door at the northern end of the room. Open the next door at the northern end of the room. Open the next door at the northeastern end of the room. Open the next door at the eastern end of the room. Open the next door at the southern end of the room. Go up the stairs to the third floor. You will return to the Mortuary later on, so you should not loot containers or speak to any NPCs.
4 Select control of only Morte. Pick up the Junk, located in the northwestern portion of this floor, and pick up the Mortuary Sanctum Key, located in the southwest portion of this floor, and open the locked door next to that container. Go down the stairs back to the second floor.
  • The dustmen are not able to speak directly to Morte, so you will be safe from getting caught.
  • Give the Mortuary Sanctum Key and Junk to the Nameless One once you have returned to the second floor.
5 Go down the stairs to the first floor. Speak to Soego. Tell him you are lost, then ask him to lead you out. He will unlock the door and let you leave the Mortuary. If the Dustman Guards speak to you, ask them if there are any guides around that can direct visitors, then thank them and they will leave you alone.
6 Travel west to the northwestern portion of the Hive, then travel north to Ragpicker's Square. Enter the midwife's hut and speak to Old Mebbeth. Say that you have some questions for her. Ask her if she is a witch and ask her to teach you magic. Answer that you need power.
  1. She will give you a Black-Barbed Seed and send you to the market. Travel to the southwestern portion of the Hive and speak to the Hive Merchant that mentions fruits, herbs, and spices. Travel southeast to the southeastern portion of the Hive. Speak to Mourns-for-Trees, located directly east of the exit leading to the northeastern portion of the Hive. Return to Old Mebbeth. Accept your level up before continuing with the quest.
  2. Return to the market in the southwestern portion of the Hive and speak to Giscorl, located near the previous Hive Merchant you spoke to. After you get Mebbeth's Wash, return to Old Mebbeth.
  3. Return to the market again and speak to Kossah-Jai, located near the previous 2 merchants you spoke to, then speak to Meir'am, located to the south. Then return to the merchants and speak to the Hive Merchant that mentions cups, plates, and tankards. Return to Meir'am with the tankard and fill it with ink. Return to Old Mebbeth and become a mage.
Once you have become a mage:
  1. Accept your level up.
  2. Equip your earrings.
  3. Copy the 3 scrolls into your spell book.
7 Travel to the southeastern portion of the Hive and into the Smoldering Corpse Bar. Do not explore the eastern portion of this area. Speak to O, located at the western half of this area, and politely ask him about the "secrets of existence" for a permanent +1 bonus to wisdom. Speak to Dak'kon and he will join your party. Speak to Dak'kon again and ask to learn the teachings of Zerthimon. Alternate using the Unbroken Circle of Zerthimon in Dak'kon's inventory and speaking to Dak'kon.
  • The correct answer to Dak'kon's questions about the Unbroken Circle of Zerthimon is always the first choice.
  • Use each Circle of Zerthimon in your inventory to add spells to your spell book. Use the 6th, 7th, and 8th Circles of Zerthimon while they are in Dak'kon's inventory.
8 Grind up 900 Copper Commons by killing respawning Hive Thugs and selling their loot. Purchase three Tattoos of Presence from Fell's Studio and equip them to the Nameless One.
9 Travel to Ragpicker's Square and use the Junk to travel through the portal located at the northern part of the map. Open the door and enter into the Trash Warrens. Anamoli will speak to you. Pick either of the first 2 options, then mention Pharod, then mention the Lady. He will let you pass peacefully. Exploring this entire area will cause additional fights. Travel to the southeastern portion of the Trash Warrens, avoiding any detours or fights along the way. Bish will speak to you. Tell him you have no name, assure him you are telling the truth, then mention Pharod. He will let you pass through the trap door into thed Buried Village.
10 Enter Pharod's Court, located at the northeast of the Buried Village. Speak to Pharod. Tell him you've come for information and that you'll help him find the Bronze Sphere in exchange for what he knows. Once you've exhausted every dialogue option, leave the court and exit the Buried Village via the southern exit to the Weeping Stone Catacombs. Barr will let you out through the locked gate. When Pharod agrees to tell you where he gets all his bodies, tell him that he is insulting the craft of magic instead of vowing or lying about not telling anyone.
11 Exploring this entire area will cause additional fights. Travel south to the southwestern exit to the Dead Nations Catacombs. Hargrimm the Bleak will speak to you. You must submit. Soego will speak to you. Speak to the Doubtful Skeleton, then return to Soego and tell him about the skeleton. Once he leaves, pry open the metal slab and read the journal. Speak to Soego again, kill him with Dak'kon, and take his skull for later. Speak to Hargrimm the Bleak again, then speak to Stale Mary, located in the southwest chamber. She will teach you Stories-Bones-Tell. Ask her to help you speak to the Silent King. She will grant you access to a portal that leads to the Silent King's throne room. Enter it. Hargrimm the Bleak will speak to you. Vow to keep quiet about the Silent King's fate in exchange for being able to leave. Exit the throne room.
12 Exit the Dead Nation to the southwest and enter the Drowned Nations Catacombs. Exploring this entire area will cause additional fights. Carefully make your way to the southeast corner by first traveling to the southwest corner and then traveling east. The Bronze Sphere can be found on the dead Dustman's corpse. Return to the Dead Nations Catacombs, then to the Weeping Stone Catacombs, then to Pharod in the Buried Village. Barr will let you back in for free, since you have the Bronze Sphere.
13 Speak to Pharod. Vow to stay your hand and make sure not to threaten him. Once Annah arrives, don't threaten her either. Accept her offer to take you to where she found your body. Once you're there, speak to Annah. She'll give you a Ring of the Traveler and flirt with you. Enter the door Annah mentioned, watch Pharod die, then immediately exit via the way you came in. Return to Pharod's Court and pick up the items that he dropped.
  • Equip the Ring of the Traveler to Annah, as her AC is the worst.
14 Travel to the Gathering Dust Bar, located in the northeastern portion of the Hive. Speak to Emoric about joining the Dustman Faction and he will send you on some quests.
  1. Speak to Norochj, who is also in the Gathering Dust Bar. He'll send you to the southwestern portion of the Hive to speak to Ash-Mantle, located towards the southeast exit. Once you say farewell, he'll try to pick your pocket. Observe his technique and then let him escape. Return to Norochj then return to Emoric.
  2. Speak to Awaiting-Death, who is also in the Gathering Dust Bar. Ask him why he hasn't killed himself yet, then offer to kill him. Release him once he starts to claw at your hands and allow him to keep his money. Return to Emoric.
  3. Speak to Sere the Skeptic, who is also in the Gathering Dust Bar. She is easy to convince. Return to Emoric.
  4. Emoric will ask about Soego, who you previously found in the Dead Nations Catacombs.
  5. Promise that you'll serve the Dustmen and their ideals and you will join the faction.
Avoid mentioning your immortality to Emoric, or he will kick you out of the Dustman faction.
15 Begin completing quests in the Hive, Mortuary, and underground areas.
  1. Speak to Ingress, located in the northeastern portion of the Hive. Then travel to the Smoldering Corpse Bar and speak to Candrian. He will give you a Negative Token if you ask him about the Negative Material plane. Equip it to the Nameless One. Ask him about Ingress and then return to Ingress. Then again return to Candrian and get Ingress' Teeth. Use the item and tell it you want it to become a magical weapon. Equip it to Morte.
  2. Speak to Barkis, located in the Smoldering Corpse Bar. Get your eye from him for 300 Copper Commons and replace your eye with it, then ask him about the other patrons to get a quest. Speak to Mochai and give her 100 Copper Commons to cover her tab. Speak to Barkis for your reward.
  3. Speak to Mar, located in the northwestern portion of the Hive, to get a box. Take the box to Ku'atraa, located in the southeastern portion of the Hive. Take the box to Brasken, located in the southwestern portion of the Hive. Take the box to Shilandra, located in the northeastern portion of the Hive. Keep the box for later.
  4. Speak to any Harlot, located within the Hive. Say "Oh?" and then "Not today, thanks." to get Morte to ask you to sport him some jink. Then insult the harlot to upgrade Morte's Litany of Curses.
  5. Purchase Embalming Fluid and Needle and Thread from the Dustmen Vault. Return to the northeastern room of the second floor of the Mortuary and speak to Ei-Vene for a permanent +1 bonus to HP.
  6. Speak to Deionarra, located in the ground floor of the Mortuary, to learn Raise Dead.
  7. Speak to Fleece, located in the northwestern portion of the Hive. Say farewell and observe his pickpocketing technique.
  8. Speak to Arlo, located in the northwestern portion of the Hive. Ask him about Nestor and offer to get rid of him. Speak to Nestor and offer to get him his fork. Speak to One-Ear outside the building, ask him how he lost his ear, and lie about your powers over the dead. Return to Nestor for a reward, then return to Arlo for another reward. Equip the Obsidian Earring to Annah.
  9. Speak to Marrow-Friend, located in Ragpicker's Square. Let him eat a little bit of you, then ask him about the finger bone. Stop him from gnawing on it by offering yourself instead in exchange for the finger bone. Use the finger bone to get Mempa's Biting Ring. Equip the ring to Annah.
  10. Speak to Baen the Sender, located in the northeastern portion of the Hive, and offer to deliver his message. Speak to Craddock, located in the southwestern portion of the Hive. Speak to Jhelai, located in the southeastern portion of the Hive. Return to Craddock and tell him the truth about what Jhelai said to upgrade Morte's Litany of Curses.
  11. Speak to Creeden, located in front of the Office of Vermin and Disease Control, about Morte and mimirs.
  12. Speak to all your party members.
16
  • Purchase various things from various shops before moving on. Farm the respawning enemies in the Alley of Dangerous Angles until you have purchased every item in this step. You may take as long as you want and rest as many times as you want for free in the Open Tomb, located in the northeastern portion of the Hive.
  • Dustmen Vault, accessed by speaking to Emoric.
    • Bone Dagger. Equip to the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Greater Embalming x2. Teach to Dak'kon and the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Hold Undead x2. Teach to Dak'kon and the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Identify. Teach to Dak'kon.
    • Scroll of Minor Embalming x2. Teach to Dak'kon and the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Remove Curse x2. Teach to Dak'kon and the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Shroud of Shadows x2. Teach to Dak'kon and the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Vampiric Touch x2. Teach to Dak'kon and the Nameless One.
  • Dver's Concoctions, accessed in the southwestern portion of the Hive.
    • Scroll of Blur. Teach to the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Luck. Teach to the Nameless One.
  • Fell's Studio. Annah will be afraid to speak to Fell, but can be easily calmed down.
    • Tattoo of Action and Tattoo of Greater Action for dexterity. Equip to Annah.
    • Tattoo of Greater Health that can give Dak'kon health regeneration.
    • Tattoo of Greater Presence for charisma. Equip to the Nameless One.
    • Tattoo of Health for the Nameless One's health regeneration when resting is not an option.
    • Tattoo of Insight and Tattoo of Revelation for intelligence.
    • Tattoo of Might and Tattoo of Greater Might for strength. Equip to Dak'kon.
    • Tattoo of the Art for the Nameless One.
    • Tattoo of the Spirit and Tattoo of the Soul for wisdom.
  • Old Mebbeth's shop, accessed in Ragpicker's Square.
    • Scroll of Blindness. Teach to the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Blood Bridge. Teach to Dak'kon.
    • Scroll of Blur. Teach to Dak'kon.
    • Scroll of Chromatic Orb. Teach to Dak'kon.
    • Scroll of Friends. Teach to the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Horror. Teach to the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Knock. Teach to the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Luck. Teach to Dak'kon.
    • Scroll of Magic Missile. Teach to the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Pacify. Teach to the Nameless One.
    • Scroll of Swarm Curse. Teach to the Nameless One.
17 Enter the Tenement of Thugs, located in the southeastern portion of the Hive. Exploring this entire area will cause additional fights. Beware of traps! Avoid the fights by opening the door located to your southwest, then open the next 2 doors you encounter leading southeast. Skip the 2 side doors and open the door at the end of the hall. Open the eastern door, take the Iron Prybar from the container, and go up the stairs. Avoid the fight by using Annah's stealth to go up the stairs. Fight the 4 enemies and retrieve the key. Return to the first floor and open the southwest door, then the northwest door, then run quickly out the western exit.
18 Enter the southwest building, use Stories-Bones-Tell on the dead Dabus, and take the Hammer. Exit the building and exit the area via the southeast exit. Speak to the Dabus in the Alley of Lingering Sighs and tell it the truth about the dead Dabus. Return to the face on the wall. Undo the Dabus repairs and then return again to the face and you'll enter the Lower Ward. Make sure Morte has no items in his inventory before finishing this step.
19 Pick up any items that Morte may have dropped. Enter the Wrecked House, located on the southern portion of this area, and go down the stairs. Morte and Lothar will speak to you and be courteous to them both. Then open the divan, located in the center of the room, and enter it. Enter the portal located in the southeastern corner of this area. Exploring this entire area will cause additional fights. Travel south to the southwestern exit to the Dead Nations Catacombs, then travel to the southwestern exit to the Drowned Nations Catacombs. Select only the Nameless One and travel to the southeastern corner of this area and enter the tomb.
20 Complete the Tomb of the Nameless One by attempting to enter the central room. You will be teleported to each of the 3 side rooms, where you must pick up a key and then step on the symbol of torment to get killed and teleported back to the entrance of the tomb. Once you have all 3 keys and enter the central chamber, being reading the various inscriptions on the walls. Once you have read all of them, the central sarcophagus will contain the last key. Exit the room via the southwest exit and you'll get teleported to the final room. Take at least the Bloody Teardrop and the Scroll of Ax of Torment. Exit through the portal. Return the way you came from Lothar's and speak to him. Exit the building to get Morte back in your party.
  • Equip Ingress' Teeth to Morte.
  • Speak to Annah and Morte after completing this step for additional dialogue options.
